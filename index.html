  • There are 2 players , player 1 plays first
  • When he rolls ,a random number from 1 to 6 is generated which is displayed by the image
  • A player is free to roll a number as many times as he wishes as long as he does not get a 1
  • If the player gets a 1 then all his current score is lost and now its the other players turn
  • After rolling once, if a player chooses to roll again then the next roll is also added to the current box
  • The score gets added to the current box until the player chooses to hold (in which case the current score is added to total score) or if he gets a 1(in which case all his current score is lost)
  • The player who first reaches 100 points is the winner
  • If you want to play a new game without this dialog box popping up, please use "New Game" button
